Accessing labs and lecture notebooks

  • Labs are accessed entirely in your web browser. Go to the lab website, data8.berkeley.edu.
  • Log in with your @berkeley.edu account. (If you’re not logged in, you should see the CalNet login page.)
  • Click the green Start Server button.
  • Go back to this page and click the link to the lab or notebook you want to work on. This will give you your own copy of that notebook, and then open it for you.
  • To come back later to a notebook you’ve already started, click the link to it again.
